My name is Artem Gordeev. I am a choreographer from Kyiv, Ukraine. My life purpose is to serve people through the art of choreography. My works are useful, timely and relevant to a particular place and moment.
'ANIMA', One-act contemporary ballet

Premiere
April 12, 2014
Kyiv State Municipal Academic Opera and Ballet Theatre, 'KMATOB-FEST-2014'

Company
LITSO Dance Company

Music
L'Orchestra de Contrebasses, Marseille, France

Costume Designer
Zhenia Kirienko
'Anima' is a spiritual experience for me. The title of the work 'Anima' means soul, and it appeared for a reason. The main character faces challenges which he is bound to accept, overcome and move forward. This is a dance of the destiny or a dance with the destiny. The action takes place beyond time and space, in the spheres of the eternal soul.

'Anima' is a central part of the trilogy and the first one I have created. The first part, 'Forma', is physical life, matter. The third part, 'Spiritus', is the spirit, direct force.

In a way, this experience has shaped myself as a professional and a master in terms of a formed artistic taste.

Kazimir Malevich is a rock, a man from Space. It took me a while to realize it. And he is a rock of the world art. He is a source of what I feel is right and important, and namely innovation, progress, scale of creative personality.

Suprematism is the top of the rock, which gave a start to so many artists, creators, inspired so many extraordinary works of art. Suprematism is a point of reference, and I find it comparable to the ballet "The Rite of Spring" by Vaslav Nijinsky in its magnitude and power.

'Sounds of the House' choreographic performance

Premiere
May 18, 2015
Literature-Memorial Museum to Mikhail Bulgakov

Company
Dancers of the international ballet project 'The Great Gatsby Ballet' by Dwight Rhoden (Complexions Contemporary Ballet, USA)

Music
'Sounds of the House' suite by Viktoriia Polevaya

Costumes
Fedor Vozianov
For me this performance is the sound of Bulgakov's house where he lived in his youth in Kyiv. After visiting the newly-opened museum back in 1994, the famous Ukrainian composer Victoriia Polevaya created beautiful music which embodied its special, mystical atmosphere and amazed me with the lack of conventional musical structure.

The house itself carries a strong historical component, as a period of time when Bulgakov lived in Kyiv was filled with a large number of collisions and changes in society, which are very much aline with the present time. Therefore, it was a powerful message for us to create this work.

'Master of Time, or everyone's got talent'

Premiere
January 2-10, 2014

Company
LITSO Dance Company

Music
Various composers

Costume Designer
Zhenia Kirienko

A fairytale for children and adults, musical show with the elements of choreography, circus and drama.

This production was very much inspired by the aesthetics of the street, theatricality, farce, circus. Since ancient times artists have been performing in the squares to bring people joy and happiness. We have tried to show all these in movements, costumes, make-up, drama. Circus artists are magicians, they can easily turn into trolls, leprechaun, elves. I managed to show this big, vast world of the artist that captured the attention and love of young audience.

'Malevich 0,10.02' choreographic show

Premiere
June 12, 2016

Company
Check point dance project participants

Music
Structural music improvisation by 'Supremus' group

Costumes
Fedor Vozianov
This project was rather special for me as we have tried a new approach to the choreography creation, and namely process dance facilitation. The peculiarity of this approach is that the dance is born from the dancer's movement channel in the context of the phenomenal field. The main task of a choreographer is to help the performer to channel and dance their inner process, as well as to fill this work with artistic expression, awareness and creative sense.

A black square, cross and circle by Kazimir Malevich have been used as symbols of a man, his destiny and the Earth.

Creativity for me is the natural movement, a force that is able to move people forward, inspire and enlighten them.

Talent comes from the indefinite world, a spiritual metaphysical power. But what counts with any gift or talent is how you use it, not what kind of nature it has, or what others think of it. What you do with it is the determining factor and your responsibility.

Realization is when your ideas come to life, when there is a plan and you can follow it. Realization is the speed of existing in structure and matter.

The process of creating a ballet is time-consuming, unyielding. Golden thread, crossing of muscles and music, movement and rhythm. The dance and musical score merge together and resemble a DNA rose, with its petals overlapping and rotating.

Dance is like circles. Circles of life, whirlwind, the fate spinning. Dance is actually everything existing out there and much deeper than we are used to think of it.

Oddly enough, music is also movement for me, like a wave. I hear music and instantly feel the dynamics. This power is compared to a dance, which in its turn, makes this power more soft, soothing, agreeable with a viewer.

Art is when you don't need to lie. This is your truth. The quintessence of all your life choices, everything you have managed to absorb and accumulate.
